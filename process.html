Our Process

From first call to final walkthrough — here's exactly what to expect.

Why Process Matters

Most remodeling headaches come from poor process, not poor skills.

We've heard the same stories: the contractor who stopped showing up, the estimate that tripled, the project that sat unfinished for months. Those aren't skill problems — they're process problems.

We built a process that eliminates those problems. Clear expectations, daily communication, and a team that shows up and finishes what they start.

The Seven Steps

1

First Conversation

Call us at 910-363-4073 or send a message through the contact form. Trista will follow up within one business day to learn about your project, answer initial questions, and schedule a site visit at your convenience. We'll be upfront about whether it's a good fit for what we do.

Typical follow-up time: Next business day. We don't let inquiries sit.

2

Site Visit

We come to your home. Walking the space matters — it's how we understand what's structurally possible, where the challenges are, and what changes will make the biggest impact. This visit is at no charge and carries no obligation.

3

Estimate & Budget Discussion

After the site visit, we put together a clear, itemized estimate. We walk through every line item with you — no vague allowances, no buried costs. If something is uncertain, we say so and explain why.

Typical kitchen remodel range: $55,000–$65,000, depending on scope and materials. We'll tell you where your project falls early in the conversation.

4

Product Selections

Cabinet styles, door profiles, countertop materials, hardware, paint colors — there are a lot of decisions in a kitchen remodel. We guide you through the ones that matter most, help you see how choices work together, and make sure you're confident in your selections before anything is ordered.

5

In-House Design & Layout

We finalize the layout and design so everything is decided before demo starts. This is where we work out exact dimensions, cabinet placement, appliance locations, and any structural changes. Getting this right upfront is what keeps the project on schedule.

6

The Build Phase

Work begins. Our team shows up on schedule and communicates with you daily throughout the build. You'll always know what happened today and what's happening tomorrow. If anything unexpected comes up, we talk to you about it immediately — no surprises waiting at the end.

Quality checks: We review our work at each phase before moving to the next, so issues are caught early and corrected immediately.

7

Final Walkthrough

When the work is done, we walk the finished kitchen with you together. We don't consider a project complete until you do. If something isn't right, we fix it before we leave. This walkthrough is our commitment to the quality of the final product.
