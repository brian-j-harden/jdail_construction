Meet the Owner

Jason Dail, Owner & Licensed General Contractor

Jason Dail started Coastal Kitchens NC after two decades of construction work across Brunswick County. He's a licensed general contractor — which means the team can handle structural modifications that most kitchen remodelers have to subcontract out or walk away from.

The company runs as J. Dail Construction, doing business as Coastal Kitchens NC, and operates as a true turnkey contractor: one team, one contract, one GC license covering design, demo, build, and finish. Homeowners don't need to coordinate between a designer, a cabinet company, and a general contractor — that's handled in-house, start to finish.

Jason built the business around the things customers told him mattered most: knowing what a project will cost upfront, getting calls returned, and having a crew that shows up on schedule and communicates while they're there.

Our Values

What We Actually Believe About Remodeling

These aren't marketing lines. They're the things we've learned from doing this for two decades.

"Budget clarity matters more than the lowest bid."

A low estimate that doubles mid-project costs more than an honest estimate upfront — financially and emotionally. We present clear numbers and stand behind them.

"The build phase shouldn't feel like a black box."

Homeowners shouldn't have to wonder what's happening in their own kitchen. We communicate daily — even when the answer is "everything is on track."

"A team that works together does better work."

We don't assemble a new crew for each project. The people who work on your kitchen have worked alongside each other for years. That consistency shows in the result.
