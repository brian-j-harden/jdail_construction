Shaker Blue

Soft. Sophisticated. Adaptable.

Pearl Gray

Pearl Gray sits between warm and cool — soft enough to feel welcoming, neutral enough to work with a wide range of countertop and hardware choices. It's the quieter, more understated alternative to white that still photographs beautifully and holds up well over time.

A traditional full-overlay profile with clean, defined lines and a more formal character than standard shaker. The face-frame construction gives the kitchen a polished, finished look that pairs well with marble, quartz, or painted wood countertops — and reads as intentionally designed rather than simply updated.

Dramatic. Current. High-contrast.

Charcoal Gray

The boldest option in the lineup. Charcoal cabinets make a deliberate statement and pair most dramatically with light quartz or marble countertops and brushed nickel or matte black hardware. Trending strongly in high-end coastal renovations — and for good reason. It photographs strikingly and holds up well over time.
