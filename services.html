Our Services

Full-scope kitchen remodeling — from design through final installation.

Most Requested

Kitchen Remodels

A full kitchen remodel is one of the highest-return projects you can do on a coastal North Carolina home. We manage the entire scope — from the first site visit through the final walkthrough — with clear communication at every step.

Our typical kitchen remodel runs $55,000–$65,000 depending on scope, materials, and any structural changes. We're transparent about cost from day one.

  • Initial design consultation and layout planning
  • Demolition and structural modifications as needed
  • Cabinet design, sourcing, and installation
  • Countertop selection and installation
  • Lighting, plumbing, and finishing work
  • Final walkthrough and quality review

Cabinetry

The right cabinets make or break a kitchen. We work with custom and semi-custom cabinet lines that fit the style of coastal and transitional homes — without the cost of fully custom millwork.

  • Cabinet style selection and space planning
  • Semi-custom and custom cabinet sourcing
  • Professional installation with attention to level and fit
  • Hardware selection and installation
  • Interior accessories (pull-outs, organizers, etc.)

Countertops

Countertops are one of the first things people notice in a kitchen. We help you select the right material — quartz, granite, or other premium surfaces — and handle sourcing and installation so the seams are tight and the finish is right.

  • Material and finish consultation
  • Quartz, granite, and natural stone options
  • Precise templating and fabrication coordination
  • Professional installation and edge detailing
  • Backsplash coordination

Bathroom Remodels

We apply the same full-service approach to bathrooms that we bring to kitchens — in-house design, licensed GC oversight, daily communication, and a final walkthrough before we leave. One team, one contract, no juggling subcontractors. Minimum project size: $20,000.

  • Design consultation and layout planning
  • Demolition and structural modifications as needed
  • Tile, vanity, and fixture selection and installation
  • Plumbing and lighting coordination
  • Quality checks at each phase
  • Final walkthrough and quality review

Partial Upgrades

Not every kitchen needs a full remodel. Sometimes the right move is a targeted improvement — new countertops and a sink, updated lighting, or a fresh backsplash. We'll be honest with you about what will make the biggest difference.

  • Countertop and sink replacement
  • Backsplash installation
  • Lighting upgrades
  • Hardware and fixture updates
  • Individual cabinet or door replacement
