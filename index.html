Why Choose Us

Most Remodeling Headaches Come From Bad Process, Not Bad Skill

The same complaints follow bad remodeling companies everywhere: the estimate that doubled, the contractor who stopped showing up, the project that never quite finished. Those aren't skill problems — they're process problems. We built Coastal Kitchens NC around fixing them: a licensed GC running the full scope in-house, clear numbers before work starts, and a team that communicates daily from the first day to the final walkthrough.

  • 20 years of experience across coastal North Carolina
  • True one-stop shop — one GC license covers design, demo, build, and finish
  • Clear, upfront budget discussions before any work begins
  • Daily communication throughout the build phase — no guessing
  • A consistent team — not a rotating cast of unfamiliar subcontractors
  • Solid wood construction built for coastal humidity — not materials that fail inland
  • Most projects completed in 2–4 weeks with minimal disruption

How It Works

From First Call to Final Walkthrough

Most of our kitchen remodels take 2–4 weeks. Here's exactly what working with us looks like at every step.

01

First Conversation

Call or send a message. Trista follows up within one business day to learn about your project and schedule a site visit.

02

Site Visit

We come to you. Walking the space helps us understand what's possible and what you actually need.

03

Estimate & Budget

No guessing games. We present a clear estimate and walk through every line before any work begins.

04

Product Selections

Cabinet styles, countertop materials, color palettes — we guide you through the choices, not overwhelm you.

05

Design & Layout

We create a layout that fits your space, your style, and the way you actually use your kitchen.

06

The Build

Scheduling, daily communication, quality checks. You always know where things stand and what's next.

07

Final Walkthrough

We walk the finished kitchen with you and don't leave until everything is exactly right.

Typical timeline: Most kitchen remodels are completed in 2–4 weeks. We schedule carefully and communicate any changes immediately.
